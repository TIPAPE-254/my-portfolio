## About the Platform

UNITY WITHIN is a youth-centered mental wellness platform designed to make emotional support accessible, safe, and stigma-free. Built with a human-first approach, it combines AI-assisted companionship, self-therapizing tools, and anonymous peer support to help individuals understand, express, and manage their mental wellbeing.

The platform enables users to track moods, journal through text or voice, practice grounding and breathing exercises, and connect anonymously with others‚Äîwhile maintaining strong privacy and ethical safeguards. At its core is BUDDIE, an empathetic AI companion that offers non-judgmental support, gentle coping strategies, and encourages healthy reflection without replacing professional care.

UNITY WITHIN is designed to scale across campuses and communities, supporting early intervention, mental health research, and culturally relevant wellbeing solutions‚Äîstarting in Kenya, with a vision for global impact.
